“Text” was a Republishing assignment. Choosing a 1000 word text, considering it’s entirety, and Republishing it fluid, but new way. I choose a text from one of my favorite books, The Love of Impermanent Things by Mary Rose O’Reilly. This text is all about the transperency of self and our connect to nature. Using the free hand embroidery sewing machine technique, I hand sewed the text onto a thin, transparent farbric. .
