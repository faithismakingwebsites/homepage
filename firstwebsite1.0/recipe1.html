dad's chili recipe

  • 1 lb of ground beef

  • 1 16oz (or 15 oz) dark red kidney bean
  • 1 16 oz (or 15 oz) light red kidney beans
  • 16 oz to 24 oz of chunky salsa

    how to
  1. Open cans of kidney beans drain water and pour beans in a pot
  2. In a skillet brown the beef
  3. Add beef to pot with beans
  4. Pour 2/3 rds of your salsa into pot and mix Decide how much more to add based on how thick u make it.
  5. Put on stove bring to a boil and stir once boiling stir, cut heat to low or simmer put a top/cover on pot and cook for 30 to 45 minutes- stir every 10 minutes or so
